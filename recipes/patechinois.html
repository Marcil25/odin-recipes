Paté Chinois

Paté chinois, quick and simple


The paté chinois is a typical meal from Québec. It is made with simple ingredients layed on top of each other.


Ingredients

  • 1 Can of creamy corn
  • 1 can of corn
  • 500g of Ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 4-5 Potatoes
  • 1/2 cup of Milk
  • 2 Tbsp of Butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Pre-heat over to 400 F
  2. Peel the patatoes and dice in 1 inch pieces. Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook the potatoes until soft (10-15 minutes).
  3. While the potatoes are cooking, dice an onion and cook the ground beef in a pan. Add the onion before the beef is fully cook. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Once the beef is cooked, put aside.
  5. When the potatoes are cooked, drain from the pot. Mash the potatoes with a potatoes masher, add the butter then the milk. Mix until the mixture is smooth and creamy. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. In a dish, add at the bottom the cooked ground beef.
  7. On top of the beef, add the creamy corn and the regular corn.
  8. Add the mashed potatoes on top.
  9. Cook in the oven for 15 min, or until the potatoes are brown.
