Poutine Maison

Cheese, Fries and Gravy: Poutine


With its soft cheese curds or crispy fries, the Poutine will make his eater really happy.


Ingredients

  • 1 bag of Cheese curds
  • Fresh fries, or over baked fries
  • Gravy (can from St-Hubert)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Put the fries in a dish. Add salt to taste
  2. Add about 1/3 to 1/2 of the sauce on the fries.
  3. Add the cheese curds.
  4. Add the remaining of the sauce.
  5. Enjoy !
